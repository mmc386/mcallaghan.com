Matt Callaghan, MD

About Me



Hi, my name is Matt Callaghan. I am an Interventional Radiologist born and raised in the Philadelphia area. As an IR I specialize in minimally invasive image guided procedures which often obviate the need for traditional surgery. My professional interests include intervetional oncology, thromboembolic disease, hepatobiliary disease, and the intersection between Radiology and Computer Science/AI.
